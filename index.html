PANGOLIN

Pangolin is a free tool designed for modern guitarists. It helps you explore scales, chords and keys through an interactive visualization directly on the guitar fretboard. Intuitive, fast and accessible from any device.

Fretboard Visualizer

In the “Fretboard Visualizer” section you can select the key, the musical scale and the number of strings of your guitar. The system shows you the notes of the scale directly on the virtual fretboard. You can customize the tuning of each string to suit your instrument.

Chord Construction

In the “Chord Construction” section you can build chords by selecting intervals starting from each degree of the scale. You can view the selected notes in real time on the fretboard and experiment with the sound of each chord within the chosen modal context.
