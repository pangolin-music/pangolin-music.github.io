Privacy Policy

Last update: May 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how personal information is collected, used, and shared when you visit or use Pangolin - Guitar Fretboard Visualizer, available at https://pangolin-music.github.io/.

Collected Information

We do not directly collect personally identifiable information. However, anonymous data may be collected via:

  • Technical cookies: to ensure the proper functioning of the site
  • Google AdSense: for personalized advertising

Use of Cookies

We use both first-party and third-party cookies. In particular, Google AdSense may use the DoubleClick cookie to display personalized ads based on your browsing activity.

For more information, please visit the official Google Ads page.

Disabling Cookies

You can disable cookies by changing your browser settings or by visiting YourOnlineChoices to manage your advertising preferences.

Google AdSense

This site displays ads via Google AdSense. Information collected by Google is subject to its own privacy policy. You can manage or disable personalized advertising through the Ads Settings.

User Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your data. For any requests, please contact us at:
